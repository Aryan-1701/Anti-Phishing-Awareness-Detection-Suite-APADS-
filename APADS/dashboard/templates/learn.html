How to Spot Phishing

Home Reports
  • Check the sender address carefully — display name can be spoofed.
  • Hover (or long-press) links to see the real domain before clicking.
  • Look for urgent language asking to verify/update credentials.
  • Do not open attachments from unknown or unexpected senders.
  • Enable 2FA on critical accounts and use app passwords for integrations.

Mini Quiz (for awareness)

Q: You receive "Your account is suspended" with a link — what do you do?

  1. Click link immediately — risky
  2. Open a new browser tab and manually visit the official site — safe ✅
